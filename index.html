About STEM Quest Learning Lab

STEM Quest Learning Lab runs curriculum-aligned learner programs and teacher workshops. We teach Foundation, Intermediate and Senior phase learners with a balance of unplugged activities, block-based coding and physical computing. Our goal: help learners grow naturally, think computationally and love building things that work.

Programs by Phase

Age-appropriate sequences that build year-on-year.

Foundation Phase

Unplugged robotics, Scratch Jr storytelling, and simple maker activities to introduce sequencing & logic.

Intermediate Phase

Scratch 3 projects, BBC micro:bit sensor builds and TinkerCAD modelling to explore variables and loops.

Senior Phase

Advanced Arduino projects, LEGO Mindstorms/SPIKE challenges and capstone projects for competitions.

Popular Workshops

Short, practical and hands-on — perfect for learners and teacher training days.

Unplugged Robotics

No devices required — algorithmic thinking with games and physical activities. Great for Foundation & early Intermediate.

Scratch Jr & Scratch 3

Storytelling, animations and games — block coding to explore sequencing, events and variables.

BBC micro:bit

Intro to sensors, displays and radio — a simple path from blocks to Python.

Arduino Projects

Basic circuits, sensors and actuators — learn to code and build physical devices.

LEGO Robotics

Design, program and test LEGO robots for fun challenges and competitions.

TinkerCAD & 3D Modelling

Simple 3D design and electronics simulation — great for maker projects.
